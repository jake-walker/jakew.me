# About

Hey, I'm Jake, a Site Reliability Engineer based in the UK.

I've been tinkering with computers since I was old enough to break one. That early curiosity turned into a career in tech, starting with a cyber security apprenticeship in 2020 and eventually landing me in SRE, where I spend my days making sure things don't fall over.

Outside of work I'm usually deep in a side project - whether that's building an app, designing a PCB, writing code, or self-hosting something I probably didn't need to self-host. I share what I'm working on here, partly to document it for myself and partly in case it's useful to someone else.
